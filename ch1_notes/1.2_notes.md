# Key Concepts:

1. Implication

    Definition: An **implication** (or **conditional**) is a molecular statement of the form:

&& A \implies B $$

    where $A$ and $B$ are statments. We say that: 
    - $A$ is the **antecendent** (or **hypothesis**),
    - $B$ is the **consequent** (or **conclusion**).# Key Concepts:
