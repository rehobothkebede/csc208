## Quadratic Formula
$$
 x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
$$

## Binomial Theorem
$$
 (x + y)^n = \sum_{k=0}^{n} \binom{n}{k} x^{n-k} y^k
$$
